Antipasto Platter

PROCEDURES

  • Arrange a large serving platter or board.
  • Place the assorted cured meats on the platter, creating folds and layers for an appealing presentation.
  • Arrange various cheeses in different sections of the platter.
  • Fill small bowls with marinated olives, marinated artichoke hearts, and roasted red pepper slices, placing them strategically on the platter.
  • Scatter cherry tomatoes, grilled or pickled vegetables, and fresh fruit around the platter for color and variety.
  • Fill in gaps with breadsticks, crusty bread, and a variety of nuts.
  • Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze over the cheeses and vegetables.
  • Garnish with fresh herbs for a finishing touch.
  • Serve the Ultimate Antipasto Platter as a centerpiece for your gathering. Enjoy!
