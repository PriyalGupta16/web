Gol-Gappe

PROCEDURE

  • To make pani,take a big utensil and add water,tamarind paste,green
    chilli paste,mint paste,coriander paste,jeera powder,salt,and black salt.
  • Saute it for 10 minutes on medium flame.After sauteing,put pani in the
    fridge for cold.
  • Meanwhile,make a potato mixture.In a bowl add potatoes,red chana,coriander,salt,black salt, and jeera powder.Mix it well.
  • Now assemble PaniPuri.Take some puri and crack them from the top.
    Fill the potato mixture in it.Pour the Pani in each puri.
  • ENJOY YOUR GOL-GAPPE!
