Italian Red Wine

PROCEDURES

  • Harvest Grapes: Pick grapes at optimal ripeness.
  • Crush & Destem: Crush grapes to release juice; remove stems.
  • Fermentation: Yeast converts sugars to alcohol; skins impart color and tannins.
  • Pressing: Separate solids (pomace) from liquid (wine).
  • Aging: Age in oak barrels or stainless steel tanks.
  • Clarification: Remove remaining solids; clarify the wine.
  • Bottling: Fill and seal wine bottles for further aging or consumption.
