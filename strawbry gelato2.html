Strawberry Gelato

PROCEDURES

  • Prepare the Strawberries: Wash, hull, and halve the strawberries.
  • Make the Strawberry Puree: In a saucepan, combine the strawberry
    puree, granulated sugar, and lemon juice.
    Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar dissolves and the mixture thickens slightly. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Prepare the Gelato Base: In a separate bowl, whisk
    together the whole milk, heavy cream, and vanilla extract.
    Add the cooled strawberry mixture to the milk and cream mixture, stirring until well combined.
  • Chill the Mixture: Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least
    4 hours or overnight to enhance flavors
    and achieve a smoother texture.
