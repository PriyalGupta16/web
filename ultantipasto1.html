Antipasto Platter

INGREDIENTS

  • Assorted cured meats (such as prosciutto, salami, and coppa)
  • Various cheeses (such as mozzarella, brie, and aged cheddar)
  • Marinated olives (green and black varieties)
  • Marinated artichoke hearts
  • Roasted red peppers, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Grilled or pickled vegetables (zucchini, eggplant, and asparagus)
  • Fresh fruit (grapes, figs, or melon)
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pistachios)
  • Breadsticks and/or crusty bread
  • Extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze for dipping
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (such as basil and rosemary)
