Marinated Olives

PROCEDURES

  • In a bowl, combine the mixed olives, sliced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, and crushed red pepper flakes.
  • Add the lemon zest and juice to the olive mixture.
  • Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the olives and toss everything together until well combined.
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the olives marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Before serving, bring the marinated olives to room temperature.
  • Transfer the olives to a serving dish, drizzle with a bit more olive oil if desired, and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  • Serve the Marinated Olives as a flavorful appetizer or part of an antipasto platter. Enjoy!
