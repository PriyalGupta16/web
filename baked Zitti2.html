Baked Ziti

PROCEDURES

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until the onion is softened.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper. Simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Layer half of the ziti and ricotta mixture over the sauce, then top with half of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  • Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  • Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and garnish with chopped fresh basil or parsley before serving.
  • Serve the baked ziti hot and enjoy!
