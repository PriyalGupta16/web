Caprese Salad

PROCEDURES

  • Arrange the tomato slices on a serving platter.

  • Place a slice of fresh mozzarella on top of each tomato slice.

  • Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices, distributing them evenly.

  • Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.

  • If desired, drizzle balsamic glaze over the top for added flavor.

  • Season the Caprese salad with salt and black pepper to taste.

  • Serve immediately and enjoy the freshness of this classic Caprese Salad!
