Limoncello

PROCEDURES

  • Thoroughly wash and dry the lemons. Using a vegetable peeler, carefully remove the zest from the lemons, avoiding the white pith.
  • Place the lemon peels in a large glass jar or container and pour the vodka or grain alcohol over the peels, ensuring they are completely submerged.
  • Seal the jar tightly and let it sit in a cool, dark place for at least 1-2 weeks to infuse the alcohol with lemon flavor.
  • In a saucepan, combine water and sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar completely dissolves. Let the syrup cool completely.
  • Strain the infused alcohol to remove the lemon peels and discard them.
  • Combine the infused alcohol with the cooled sugar syrup. Stir well to mix.
  • Transfer the Limoncello into clean bottles or jars and store them in the refrigerator. It's best enjoyed chilled!
