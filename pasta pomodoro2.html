PROCEDURES

  • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add crushed tomatoes to the skillet, then stir in sugar, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Toss the cooked pasta into the tomato sauce, ensuring it is well coated.
  • Stir in chopped fresh basil and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and serve the Pasta Pomodoro hot.
  • Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and additional fresh basil if desired.
  • Enjoy your delicious and simple Pasta Pomodoro!
