Marinated Mozzarella
Balls

PROCEDURES

  • Place the fresh mozzarella balls in a bowl.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, and lemon zest.
  • Pour the marinade over the mozzarella balls, tossing gently to coat them evenly.
  • Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the mozzarella balls marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Before serving, bring the marinated mozzarella balls to room temperature.
  • Transfer the mozzarella balls to a serving dish, drizzle with a bit more marinade if desired, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  • Serve the Marinated Mozzarella Balls as a delightful appetizer or part of a cheese board. Enjoy!
