PROCEDURES

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Place the eggplant slices on a paper towel and sprinkle with salt.
    Let them sit for about 15 minutes to release excess moisture.
    Pat them dry with another paper towel.
  • In one bowl, whisk the eggs. In another bowl, combine breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce.
    Arrange half of the baked eggplant slices on top.
    Sprinkle with half of the shredded mozzarella.
  • Repeat the layers with the remaining eggplant and mozzarella.
    Top with additional marinara sauce.
  • Bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted
    and bubbly.
  • Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil,
    and let it cool for a few
    minutes before serving.
  • Serve the easy eggplant Parmesan with your favorite side and enjoy!
